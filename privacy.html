Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Recipe Ninja ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using Recipe Ninja, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Recipes: Custom recipes you create and add to the app
  • Favorites: Recipes you mark as favorites
  • Support Communications: Information you provide when contacting our support team

Automatically Collected Information

  • App Usage: Which recipes you view, like, and share
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app experience

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal Identity: We do not collect your name, email address, or phone number (unless you contact support)
  • Location Data: We do not access or collect your location information
  • Camera/Photos: We do not access your camera or photo library
  • Contacts: We do not access your contacts or address book

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide App Functionality: Save your favorite recipes and custom recipes
  • Improve User Experience: Understand which features are most popular
  • Provide Support: Respond to your questions and technical issues
  • App Analytics: Improve app performance and fix bugs

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Favorites and Custom Recipes: Stored locally on your device
  • App Preferences: Stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: Your personal recipe data is not uploaded to our servers

Security Measures

  • All data transmission uses industry-standard encryption
  • We implement appropriate technical safeguards to protect your information
  • Local data is protected by your device's security features

Third-Party Services

Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase for:

  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix app issues

Firebase may collect anonymous usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements:

  • Ad Personalization: Based on anonymous device identifiers
  • Data Collection: AdMob may collect device information for ad targeting

See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Your Privacy Rights

Access and Control

  • View Data: All your data is visible within the app
  • Delete Data: Use "Clear Favorites" in Settings to remove all favorite recipes
  • Disable Analytics: Contact us to opt out of analytics collection

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to Know: Request information about personal data we collect
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of data sharing (we don't share personal data)

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU, you have additional rights:

  • Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

Children's Privacy

Recipe Ninja is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Remains on your device until you delete the app or clear favorites
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous analytics data may be retained for up to 2 years
  • Support Communications: Retained for up to 1 year to provide ongoing support

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing an in-app notification for significant changes

International Data Transfers

If you use our app outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers are located.
